Hsiao-Chun Lin

PhD Candidate · Department of Atmospheric and Environmental Sciences · State University of New York at Albany · hlin21@albany.edu

I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Atmospheric and Enviromental Sciences from University at Albany - State University of New York (SUNY). I received a master of science in atmospheric sciences from National Central University in Taiwan in 2015. Currently, my research focus is on the impact of assimilating New York State Mesonet observations on the severe weather forecast under the advisement of Dr. Everette Joseph. I am also a visiting student at Mesoscale & Microscale Meteorology (MMM) Laboratory at National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR).
